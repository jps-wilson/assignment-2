Meet the Chaos Crew: The Hosts Behind A Dash’ll Do Ya

If you’ve ever wondered who’s behind the mic on A Dash’ll Do Ya, now’s your chance to meet the dynamic duo making all the design school madness—and unfiltered banter—come to life.

Jess Wilson — The Creative Tornado
Jess is a design student with a knack for turning chaos into something visually stunning. When not knee-deep in projects or pulling all-nighters tweaking gradients, Jess is the voice bringing raw honesty and humor to the show. Expect lots of laughs, a bit of existential design crises, and some seriously relatable creative struggles.

Fariha Khan — The Voice of Reason (Sort Of)
Fariha balances Jess’s whirlwind energy with thoughtful insights and a grounded perspective on life as a design student. She’s the calming force behind the scenes, but don’t be fooled—she’s got a sharp wit and plenty of hilarious stories of her own. Together, Jess and Fariha create a podcast that’s equal parts chaotic and heartfelt.

Why They Started "A Dash’ll Do Ya"
Design school is intense, and sometimes you just need to vent, laugh, and share your weirdest moments. That’s exactly what Jess and Fariha set out to do with this podcast: create a space where no topic is off-limits and everything is a little messy (but always aesthetic).

Get to Know Them
Follow along as they share behind-the-scenes stories, host wild guests, and navigate the ups and downs of creative school life. Whether you’re a fellow design student or just here for the banter, Jess and Fariha promise to keep it real and keep you entertained.


Ready to join the chaos? Hit play on the latest episode and dive in!
