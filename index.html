A Dash'll Do Ya

Hot Takes, Cold Coffees, and Design Deadlines.

A Dash’ll Do Ya is a monthly podcast by design students Jess Wilson and Fariha Khan—two barely-functional creatives serving up school chaos, personal drama, and unfiltered banter.

It’s messy. It’s honest. It’s aesthetic(ish).
Because sometimes, a dash’ll do ya.

Why You Should Listen

If you've ever stayed up until 3AM tweaking a gradient or had an existential crisis in the middle of a presentation, you'll feel right at home here.
