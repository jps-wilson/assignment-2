Hot Takes, Cold Coffees, and Design Deadlines.

A Dash’ll Do Ya is a monthly podcast by design students Jess Wilson and Fariha Khan—two barely-functional creatives serving up school chaos, personal drama, and unfiltered banter.
It’s messy. It’s honest. It’s aesthetic(ish).
Because sometimes, a dash’ll do ya.

Why You Should Listen

If you've ever stayed up until 3AM tweaking a gradient or had an existential crisis in the middle of a presentation, you'll feel right at home here. We get it-this isn't just design school, it's emotional warfare with Adobe. So whether you've got hot takes, burning questions, or just need to vent, hit us up. We're always down to chat.
